Short-Term Predictions Can Be Made For Complex Systems

Nowcasting is the prediction of the present, the very near future, and the very recent past.

With enough real-time data, we can predict the present and the future with a high degree of accuracy for certain complex systems such as meteorological systems.

Data Assimilation Is Used To Generate Predictions

Data assimilation is the process of combining observations of a system with a model of the system to produce an estimate of the current state of the system.

Data Assimilation Is Used To Generate Predictions

For example, in weather forecasting, data assimilation is used to combine real-time weather observations with ensemble predictions from physics-based models (PBMs) to produce a short-term forecast.

Data Assimilation Is Used To Generate Predictions

Mobility systems cannot be predicted effectively using PBMs because they exists at a different level of complexity to meteorological systems. Agent-based models (ABMs) are able to capture the complex behaviour of mobility systems more effictively.
